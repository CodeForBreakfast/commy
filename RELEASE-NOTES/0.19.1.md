Patch release of the commy plugin, **0.19.0 → 0.19.1**.

## Highlights

**A stalled identity mint no longer wedges a seat's `post` and `react`
indefinitely.** Acquiring a bot identity mints it through a short sequence of
Zulip minter calls (`GET /users`, `POST /bots`, reactivate, regenerate), none
of which was individually bounded. A single stalled socket therefore parked the
whole acquire on an effectively infinite wait — and because that round-trip runs
while the identity bind is write-locked, the one hang wedged *every* later
acquire, and so every `post` and `react`, with no error and no timeout. The seat
looked alive but silently answered nothing, leaving a human to cancel a hung tool
call by hand.

This release bounds the mint round-trip at 30 seconds. A healthy mint (a few
sub-second calls, with room for a call riding most of its 15s rate-limit retry
budget) is unaffected; a genuine stall now surfaces promptly as a typed
`IdentityError` the plugin layer can escalate, instead of an indefinite hang.
